Our place is located in a village about 30 km from Surat Thani City, 7 km outside the Ban Na town and 4 km from Thung Tao town. There are no frequent buses that go directly to here. It is accessible by other means of transportation listed below.


From Bangkok : the best is taking train number 173 to Banna. The train leaves at 5.35 pm and arrives Banna station at 6.30 am next morning. We can pick you up there and then drive to the jungle 7 km far from Banna.


From Malaysia : via land border crossing, if you are coming to Thailand via land you can take the bus to Hat Yai railway station and then take the ordinary train number 448 to Banna. The train leaves Hat Yai around 11 am and arrives Banna at 5.23 pm.It costs only 51 baht. We can pick you up there .


From Suratthani City : from any city in Thailand take train/bus/plane to Suratthani first and then take a mini van from Taladkaset 2 (bus terminal) to Banna. There are many van offices there but try to find the van for Banna only and you don't miss. The van leaves every hour untill 6pm. It costs 60 baht and take around an hour to Banna. We will pick you up at Banna van station.


From Krabi province : you can take a bus Krabi-Suratthani and ask the driver to drop you in Thung Tao town in front of 7/11 (supermarket), call us to pick you up there.


There are mini van between Surat Thani and Ban Na every hour, buses from Surat Thani or Krabi passes Thung Tao almost every hour, so it's easy to get to. Ban Na has a train station that connects all the way from Malaysia to Bangkok, so also easy to get here by train.  If you arrive by train, we can pick you up at Ban Na train station for free or at Suratthani train staition for extra 400 B, Suratthani airport 500 B, just let us know.
